NOVEMBER 28, 2014

The day Ray Rice won his appeal, ESPN published Janay's account of the night in Atlantic City and the aftermath. Janay admitted her apology followed the Ravens' suggested script. She also disclosed she didn't remember much of what happened in the elevator and accepted responsibility for hitting Ray.

Sports Illustrated published a summary of her account. Deadspin focused on what happened when Ray and Janay met with the NFL in June. Janay told the league it was a one-time event and she just wanted the saga to be over. This apparently played a large role in the NFL's decision to suspend Rice only two games.
