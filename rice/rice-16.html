SEPTEMBER 9, 2014

Deadspin and Sports Illustrated both published Janay Rice's statement made through Instagram in which she slammed the media for causing pain to her family and calling the situation a "horrible nightmare." ESPN also shared the post, but also had a reporter who spoke to Ray and Janay about the statement. They both expressed support for one another and Janay asked people to respect their privacy. ESPN then provided a recap of the case up until that point.
