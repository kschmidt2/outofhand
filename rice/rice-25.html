SEPTEMBER 19, 2014

Goodell finally spoke to the media, saying there were many recent examples of the NFL "doing wrong" and said he had goals of implementing a new personal conduct policy. ESPN provided a thorough summary, as well as video, of the press conference.

Sports Illustrated published a partially tongue-in-cheek column explaining that Goodell still didn't get it. The author, Michael Rosenberg, wrote that although Goodell thought the real story was about the missteps with the suspension, but that it was really about the commissioner's arrogance and lack of interest in the truth.

For its story, Deadpsin's headline read, "Goodell: I suck at my job. Now trust me," and stated that was the general message of his rpess conference.
