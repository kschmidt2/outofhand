SEPTEMBER 10, 2014

The NFL announced it would be launching an independent investigation into the Rice situation, headed by former FBI director Robert S. Mueller III. ESPN and Sports Illustrated both delivered straight news stories. Sports Illustrated also published a story about the ties between the NFL and Mueller's firm. The author wrote, "independent is hardly the way to describe the probe" and pointed out that the president of the Ravens was a partner in Mueller's firm for 31 years. Further, many former lawyers at the firm went on to jobs in-house with the NFL and its teams.
