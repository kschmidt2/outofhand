DECEMBER 1, 2014

In an interview with the Today Show, Janay accused Goodell of not being honest, as covered by ESPN. She reiterated that Ray did not mislead the commissioner and thus the indefinite suspension was arbitrary. Sports Illustrated took a similar approach.

Deadspin focused on her account of the Ravens providing her with a script for the press conference and her wanting to do the press conference because she was "ready to do anything that was going to help the situation."
