SEPTEMBER 22, 2014

Ravens owner Steve Bisciotti claimed the ESPN story was "manufactured" and that all of the anonymous sources in the story were from Rice's camp. He also said that no one would be losing a job. Deadspin broke down the press conference and pointed out that the Ravens still hadn't provided an answer as to why they didn't seek the video from inside the elevator.

ESPN stood by its reporting, with reporter Don Van Natta appearing on the network, saying they interview more than 20 sources including team and league officials.

Sports Illustrated'sstory covering the press conference did not say anything unique or noteworthy.
