FEBRUARY 13, 2015

The Ray Rice saga wrapped up on February 13, 2015, with the former Ravens running back apologizing to Baltimore and thanking the fans. Deadspin and Sports Illustrated both republished Rice's letter to fans that ran in the Baltimore Sun in which Rice wrote:

"There is no excuse for domestic violence, and I apologize for the horrible mistake I made. I hope you can find it in your heart to forgive me, and I hope to make a positive difference in people's lives by raising awareness of this issue." Rice did not get a chance with another team in 2015; the domestic violence incident ended his career.
