SEPTEMBER 15, 2014

The cover story on Sports Illustrated's September 15 issue, called The Brutal Truth, shed light on the country's problems with domestic violence. The article stated the NFL and Ravens should be embarrassed by their response to the incident, as well as the fans who cheered on Rice during practice over the summer. But the author also called out the general population for not expressing outrage over an instance of domestic violence until there was indisputable video evidence. The mishandling of the Ray Rice situation shows how much more education is needed on the issue of domestic violence, the author wrote.
