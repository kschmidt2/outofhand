MARCH 18, 2015

Deadspin published several posts after Hardy signed with the Cowboys. The first discussed the terms of his deal, which was mostly made up of incentives based on games played. The site also shared a video clip of a Dallas Sportscaster ripping into the Cowboys for signing Hardy, as well as a clip of Dallas Mayor Mike Rawlings calling the signing “a shot in the gut.”

Sports Illustrated focused on the signing from a football perspective, giving the signing a grade of B-plus. The reason the grade wasn’t higher? The potential for a PR fallout. SI also included a statement from Cowboys owner and general manager Jerry Jones in which he said, “our organization understands the very serious nature of domestic violence in our society and in our league.”

ESPN covered the news in a similar manner to Sports Illustrated and included a brief summary of the domestic violence case.
