FEBRUARY 15, 2014

Deadspin reported the news of Rice and Palmer's arrest and right away stated that a tipsterclaimed the incident was more violent than was being let on. They included tweets from witnesses in their report, as well as photos and claims from witnesses suggesting the altercation was more than a minor incident.

ESPN's story, published a day after Deadspin's and two days after the incident, reported on the arrest, mainly including quotes from Rice's attorney and the Ravens. There was nothing reported about the incident possibly being more serious.
