APRIL 22, 2015

Deadspin did not publish a story with this news, they only shared a tweet from an NFL reporter. Sports Illustrated and ESPN both published straight news stories, with ESPN going a little deeper into the NFL’s investigation, which included a failed attempt to talk to Holder.

The next day, Sports Illustrated’s MMQB column published a story discussing how the NFL now punishes players doesn’t rely on criminal convictions to proceed with severe punishments. The writer, Peter King, says the NFL had to issue this suspension in order to back up its new personal conduct policy implemented the previous fall.
